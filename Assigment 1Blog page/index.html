Why I Love Anime

From my early childhood days, I fell in love with art and design, regardless of whatever I came across, I stayed true to my passion, and my passion drove me to self-learn how to create different forms of digital designs and art. Anime, being a child and an expression of art, it's only natural I fall this hard for it tooðŸ˜….

But what interested me the most about anime was how it dives deep into understanding what it means to be human, how best to live a fulfilled life, how to endure and overcome the most difficult challenges, how to properly manage, and, utilize our gifts and skills, talks about friendship and family how best to strengthen such bonds and the importance of having such strong bonds, it also talks about unspoken emotions feeling and how emotions are extraordinary links that connect us all living things.

It in-depth explains and discusses concepts that most works of art can't reach, It draws out emotions that only real live experiences should, it's makes you feel like, you are a character in the story plot, every step of the way, it combines drawing, storytelling and sound into something truly magical, it is truly something that can't be explained but experienced. Also as designer I learn a lot from watching anime.

Anime concepts can be used in creating UI interactions Color combination ideas, they play with hard and soft colors and, have them all come out, beautiful and harmonious. And most importantly I find it entertaining
